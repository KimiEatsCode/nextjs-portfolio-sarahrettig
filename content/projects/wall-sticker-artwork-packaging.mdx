## Project Overview

Print production artist role at Lot 26 Studio in San Francisco, creating wall sticker collections and product sales sheets for big box stores. Prepared artwork for overseas printing and tested prototypes in-house.

## Key Responsibilities

- Artwork preparation for overseas printing
- Prototype testing in-house
- Sales sheet design for retail distribution
- Two brands: ADD-HERES (peel-and-stick stickers) and Adorn (vinyl decals)
